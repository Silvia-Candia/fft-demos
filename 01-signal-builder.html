1 · Signal builder + live spectrum

Any signal is a sum of sine waves. The FFT recovers which sines are present and how much of each. (Part 1)

Watch: one sine in gives one bar out, a single frequency lands in a single bin. Stack several and the time signal looks messy, but the spectrum stays clean: the FFT untangles them. The spectrum x-axis is "cycles per window" = the bin index k.
