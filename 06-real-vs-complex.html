6 · Real vs complex (I/Q) spectrum

Real input gives a mirror-symmetric spectrum (half redundant). Complex I/Q input does not: +f and −f are independent. (Part 2)

0.80
0.40

Why it matters: radar and SAR sample two channels (I and Q) to get complex input on purpose. It is the only way to tell +frequency from −frequency, e.g. a target approaching versus receding. Complex FFT/IFFT is the baseline for radar and SAR.
