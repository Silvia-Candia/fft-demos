2 · Why the FFT matters: N² vs N·logN

The cost gap between the direct DFT and the FFT widens fast as N grows. (Part 1)

The point: both curves are the same transform computed two ways. At N = 32768 the FFT does about 4000× less work. That ratio is the difference between feasible and impossible for real-time onboard processing, which is the regime real-time onboard processing needs.
